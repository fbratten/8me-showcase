## What is 8me?

8me teaches the **fundamentals of AI agent orchestration** - how to make AI systems that reliably complete tasks through persistent, verified loops.

## Why "Ralph Wiggum"?

The name comes from a methodology popularized by Geoffrey Huntley: **loop persistently until the job is done**. Like the Simpsons character who doesn't give up, this approach succeeds through determination rather than perfection.

*For the AI nerds:* This was the common approach before sophisticated orchestration frameworks. Sometimes the old ways persist because they work.
