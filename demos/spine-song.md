## About the Song

**"Coding Flow State"** celebrates the SPINE methodology - the backbone of context engineering for AI agents.

### Lyrics Reference

The song weaves together key SPINE concepts:

- **TraceScope** - Observability boundaries
- **Context Stack** - Layered context management
- **RootId, SpanId, ParentId** - Trace identifiers
- **SPINE Reactor** - The orchestration core

### Genre

A fusion of:
- Jazz (improvisational flow)
- R&B (smooth progression)
- Dubstep drops (energy bursts)

The genre mix mirrors the coding experience - smooth flow punctuated by breakthrough moments.
